My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Parent Trap

Twins seperated by divorced parents, meet 12 years later at a Summer Camp their parents send them to without knowing.

A Quiet Place

Everyone is in a lockdown due to deadly, blind, but sharp hearing creatures who kill anyone that creates noises, using their really effective sound detection ears.

Shrek

A hideous ogre is on a quest to rescue Princess Fiona, for the future king, Lord Farquaad, to in return, receive his swamp back.
